Hiking the Pacific Crest Trail

2019-11-13

In late March, 2019, Andy and I started hiking northward from the California/Mexico border. We intended to follow the Pacific Crest Trail (PCT) 2,652 miles through California, Oregon, and Wasshington, to ultimately arrive at the Washington/Canada border. Six months later, after some unexpected twists and turns, we found ourselves at the northern terminus.

You can see some highlights from the trip on my Instagram, or read more about the trail on Wikipedia.
